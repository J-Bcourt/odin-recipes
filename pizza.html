Pizza

Authentic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil and Salt

Steps

  1. Form out pizza dough into the desired shape/thickness
  2. Add a layer of pizza sauce, but leave room at the edge of the dough to form a crust
  3. Place down the mozzarella cheese onto the pizza so that it applies to all the slices of the pizza
  4. Place in a preheated oven (500 Farenheit) for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  5. Place pizza on a cooling rack and apply basil and drizzle olive oil over the pizza
  6. Enjoy!
