Grilled Cheese

Gooey Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the toast (1/2 Tbsp buter)
  2. Heat skillet to 275 degrees and add two slices of buttered bread with the buter side down
  3. Add the cheese on the bread
  4. Brown the toast until both sides are golden
  5. Cut sandwich diagonally to serve
  6. Enjoy!
