Simple Mac N' Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Half and Half
  • Shredded Chedder Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Cook the macaroni, drain, and set aside
  3. Make the roux by melting the butter, blending in flour, salt, and pepper for 2 minutes
  4. Add milk and cheese
  5. Pour into baking dish and sprinkle 1/2 cup of cheese over the top and bake
  6. Enjoy!
